Getting Started with Bob IDE

Follow these 8 simple steps to set up your IBM Bob IDE trial account and start coding with AI-powered assistance. This guide will walk you through registration, installation, and authentication.

1

Visit Bob Website

Navigate to https://bob.ibm.com/ and click on the "Get free trial" tile to begin your Bob IDE journey.

Bob welcome page with "Get free trial" option

2

Fill Up Registration Details

Complete the registration form with your details including email, password, name, and country. Once all fields are filled, click Next to proceed.

Registration form for Bob trial account

3

Enter OTP

An OTP (One-Time Password) will be sent to your registered email ID to complete the sign-up process. Enter the 7-digit code and click Submit to verify your email address.

Email verification step with OTP input

4

IBMid Created & Access Trial

Congratulations! Your IBMid has been successfully created with the provided email. Now it's time to download the IBM Bob IDE to start coding with AI assistance.

Click on "Access your trial now" to visit the IBM Bob download page where you can download the IDE for your operating system. Alternatively click here.

Trial account successfully created

5

Download & Install

Download and install the Bob IDE for your operating system (macOS, Windows, or Linux). The installation process may take a few minutes, depending on your internet speed.

Select and download Bob IDE for your platform

6

Login to Bob

Once the installation is complete, open the Bob IDE and click on "Login to Bob" to authenticate your account.

⚠️ IMPORTANT
If you are not already logged in to your IBM account, you will be redirected to the IBMid login page. Please use the same email ID and password that you used to register your Bob trial account.

Bob IDE welcome screen

IBMid login page

7

Browser Verification

Your default browser will open for W3id trial account verification. Click on "Open IBM Bob.app" to complete the authentication process and return to the IDE.

Browser verification dialog

8

Success

Congratulations! Upon successful login, you will be able to see Bob's chat window in the IDE. You're now ready to start coding with Bob.

Bob IDE successfully authenticated with chat interface
